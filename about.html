ABOUT PUSHING LIMITS

WHO IS THE INTENDED AUDIENCE?

This documentary is suited for anyone from the age of 13+ regardless of race, gender, ethnicity, sexual orientation, nationality, culture, creed, or religion.

WHAT ARE THE EXPENSES OF THE FILM?

To have a successful shoot I would be required to fly and live in LA California USA, for the duration of the shoot. Training for the shoot would require a gym membership, and the setting (home, street, gym, etc.) In addition, a special diet, a dietician, and a doctor to ensure that I am not putting my health at risk, but also for great content. As things are I use a high shoe to walk without my crutches and to go long distances like town, college, or the mall. I use my crutches.

WHAT ARE THE MILESTONES?

The three bodybuilding milestones can be summarized as follows: 1. Sit-up world record (Build-up): The goal is to break the sit-up world record by performing 3,000 sit-ups at the NFL Superbowl in Las Vegas, Nevada. The aim is to inspire the world and get Arnold Schwarzenegger's attention. 2. Push-up world record (Main goal): The objective is to set a new world record of 4,000 push-ups. The current record is 3,182 push-ups in one hour. 3. Fitness course (Bonus): The goal is to complete the final qualification in fitness and fulfill a sponsorship obligation.

OUR AWESOME

TEAM

Caleb Mutombo

Writer & Producer

Please allow me to introduce myself. I am Caleb Mutombo, born on June 26 1997, a black physically challenged bodybuilder, tech enthusiast, entrepreneur-driven young man, and a recent graduate student at Falsebay College, Cape Town, South Africa. I am originally from the Democratic Republic of Congo, but I’ve been living In South Africa for the past 22 years. My passions are bodybuilding, personal development, technology and entrepreneurship.

Neil Hermann

Director

Neil began his career in 1979 specializing in fashion, sportswear, production stills and advertising photography .During this time Neil was also involved in the Film and TV industry and began directing and shooting – as well as working on film and commercial sets and behind the scenes pics. Neil uses his talents in action/adventure sports to his work advantage. This enabled him to gain many clients in the sports arena. As a qualified PADI rescue diver he has expanded to underwater photography and filming.

Simon Ekin

WHAT IS THE PROJECT HISTORY OR CURRENT STATUS OF THE PROJECT?

I have only been featured in some media publications, these were just some short interviews asking about my history in bodybuilding. A Pilot is available.

HOW ACHIEVABLE IS THE STORY, WE WANT TO TELL?

The production of this documentary is very much within reach as it will not cost an arm and a leg since there is just one main character and no need for special effects or expensive scenery, costumes, and so on… However, it won’t be cheap either. So, maybe just an arm!

WHO WILL FUND THIS FILM?

It has come to my attention that streaming and tv networks are on the hunt for fresh new content and this documentary offers that and a whole lot more. So, I am confident that steaming and TV entertainment giants would jump in. But why stop there? How about approaching tech brands with the opportunity for them to showcase some of their products, like smartwatches phones, and sports earbuds? I think we could also add some sports clothing brand giants and of course supplement brands. However, if the brands don’t pitch, then we can go for film grants from private and government organizations.

HOW WILL THIS WORK BE DISTRIBUTED?

The documentary we create is most suited for cable and streaming services. I hope to sell full ownership with all rights to the buyer of this idea. This might not be the case with our documentary. The best places to sell this film would be at film festivals, or directly through a distributor.
